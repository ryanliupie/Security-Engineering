## Identity and Access Management

Identity and Access Management is about making sure that only the right people can access an organization's data and resources. 

### IAAA

- <b>Identification</b> - This is where the user/system claims to be something/someone. This could be in the form of an email, username, user ID, badge number or account number. The surrounding system is simply being told what the user claims to be. 

- <b>Authentication</b> - Once the user/system says who they claim to be, they can prove it in the form of a password, biometrics, security token, security key, and MFA. 

    - <i>Authenticating Systems:</i> These refer to all the mechanisms used to authenticate a user.
